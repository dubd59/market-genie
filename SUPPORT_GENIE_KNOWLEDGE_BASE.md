## ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

### Account Creation Issues

Problem: User cannot create new account
Solutions:
- Verify email address format is valid
- Check if email already exists in system
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser or incognito mode
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Check for firewall or antivirus blocking

Problem: Email verification not received
Solutions:
- Check spam and junk folders
- Verify email address was entered correctly
- Resend verification email from login page
- Wait 10-15 minutes for delivery
- Try different email provider if persistent issues
- Contact support for manual verification

Problem: Password reset not working
Solutions:
- Check spam folder for reset email
- Ensure caps lock is off when entering new password
- Password must be at least 8 characters
- Use different browser if issues persist
- Clear browser data and try again
- Verify email address is correct

### Login Problems

Problem: Cannot login with correct credentials
Solutions:
- Verify caps lock status
- Check for extra spaces in email field
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito or private browsing mode
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Check if account has been suspended
- Verify internet connection stability

Problem: Account locked or suspended
Solutions:
- Account locks automatically after 5 failed login attempts
- Wait 30 minutes before trying again
- Use password reset if unsure of credentials
- Contact support if account appears permanently locked
- Check for outstanding payment issues
- Review terms of service compliance

Problem: Two-factor authentication issues
Solutions:
- Ensure device clock is synchronized
- Try generating new code
- Check authenticator app is updated
- Use backup codes if available
- Disable and re-enable 2FA if necessary
- Contact support for emergency access

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Account Questions:

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Go to Account Settings, click Security, then Change Password. Enter your current password and new password twice.

Q: Can I have multiple users on my account?
A: Yes, depending on your subscription plan. Check your plan details or upgrade for additional user seats.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Billing Settings and click Cancel Subscription. You will retain access until the end of your billing period.

Feature Questions:

Q: What is the AI Swarm feature?
A: AI Swarm uses 6 specialized AI agents working together to automate your marketing tasks including lead generation, content creation, and email campaigns.

Q: How many leads can I generate per month?
A: Lead limits depend on your subscription plan. Check your plan details in the dashboard for specific limits.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes, you can export leads, contacts, and campaign data in CSV or Excel format from the respective sections.

Technical Questions:

Q: Why are my emails going to spam?
A: Ensure your domain is authenticated, avoid spam trigger words, include unsubscribe links, and maintain good sender reputation.

Q: How do I integrate with my CRM?
A: Go to Integrations settings, select your CRM, and follow the setup wizard to connect your account.

Q: Can I use my own domain for emails?
A: Yes, with white label plans you can set up custom domains. Contact support for setup assistance.

Billing Questions:

Q: When am I charged for my subscription?
A: Billing occurs on the same date each month as your initial subscription. Check Billing Settings for your next billing date.

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: Yes, plan changes take effect immediately. Upgrades are prorated, downgrades apply at next billing cycle.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Subscription fees are generally non-refundable, but exceptions may be considered on a case-by-case basis within 30 days.
